Recipe

500 g bone-in beef brisket
10 small red beets
0.5 cabbage
1 carrot
1 parsley root
1 onion
4 potatoes
2-3 tbsp concentrated tomato paste
3-4 cloves of garlic
40 g lard
20 g salted lard
1 green bell pepper
2-3 bay leaves
salt and black pepper
citric acid to taste
sugar
water

Red Soup

Serves four people

Step 1: To prepare the beets

Peel all the beets and cut into cubes. Place the stew dish on low heat, and add lard and one tablespoon of tomato paste. Pour a couple of glasses and simmer over low heat for 15 minutes. After 15 minutes, pour some hot water and bring it to a boil, cool and strain. To preserve the beautiful red color and texture, add a pinch of citric acid to the beets.

Step 2: Cook the vegetables

Cut the cleaned carrots, parsley, and onion into strips, place them in the pan, and let them simmer for 10 minutes on low heat. Add the cabbage, peeled potatoes and seeded peppers to the boiling broth. When it starts to boil again, add the stewed beets.

Step 3: Season and simmer

After a quarter of an hour, add species and season with salt and sugar. Cook on low heat for about 20 to 30 minutes. Before we stop cooking for five minutes, we also need to cut the bacon into cubes, and we need to add the remaining tomato paste, and small cubes of bacon and garlic smashed with lard.

Step 4: Finishing the soup

Once the cooking is almost done and before we serve anything, add the beetroot infusion and pieces of meat. Bring to a boil briefly and leave it on the stove for a couple of minutes.

Step 5: Serve

Now your red soup, also known as borscht soup, is ready and ready to serve with sour cream.
